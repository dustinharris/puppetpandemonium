Digital Rain FX
=================

This asset features 5 scenes of digital rain effects.

The idea of this asset was to provide an animated backdrop for menus. The asset also includes a 3D version of the effect to use in a game world.

Feel free to contact me if you need help with the asset or if you have problems with particle effects.

=================
Usage
=================

Feel free to build on the demo scenes as they are easy to modify.

In the demo scenes you should find a Canvas which can be deleted to get rid of the scene selection menu in the bottom right.
